Do I Qualify for a Refund?

You generally qualify if:

  • You worked in the US on a J1 or H2B visa.
  • Federal or State income tax was withheld from your pay.
  • You were a "Nonresident Alien" for tax purposes.
  • Bonus: Most J1s qualify for a refund of Social Security & Medicare (FICA) taxes if withheld in error!
